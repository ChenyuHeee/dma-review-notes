## 1. Valid Arguments (有效论证)

**Definition**: An **argument** (论证) in propositional logic is a sequence of propositions. All but the final proposition are **premises** (前提). The last statement is the **conclusion** (结论).

**Valid**: The argument is valid if the premises imply the conclusion. i.e., 
$$(p_1 \land p_2 \land \dots \land p_n) \to q$$
is a **tautology**.

> 有效性不要求前提为真，只要求"如果前提为真，则结论必为真"。

**Argument form** (论证形式): 将命题变量替换为具体命题后依然有效的形式。

**Inference rules** (推理规则): 用于构建复杂论证的基本有效论证形式。

## 2. Rules of Inference for Propositional Logic

### 2.1 Modus Ponens (假言推理/肯定前件式)

$$\begin{array}{c}
p \\
p \to q \\
\hline
\therefore q
\end{array}$$

- 对应的永真式: $(p \land (p \to q)) \to q$

**Example**:
- $p$: "It is snowing."
- $q$: "I will study discrete math."
- "If it is snowing, then I will study discrete math."
- "It is snowing."
- $\therefore$ "I will study discrete math."

---

### 2.2 Modus Tollens (取拒式/否定后件式)

$$\begin{array}{c}
\neg q \\
p \to q \\
\hline
\therefore \neg p
\end{array}$$

- 对应的永真式: $(\neg q \land (p \to q)) \to \neg p$

**Example**:
- "If it is snowing, then I will study discrete math."
- "I will not study discrete math."
- $\therefore$ "It is not snowing."

---

### 2.3 Hypothetical Syllogism (假言三段论)

$$\begin{array}{c}
p \to q \\
q \to r \\
\hline
\therefore p \to r
\end{array}$$

- 对应的永真式: $((p \to q) \land (q \to r)) \to (p \to r)$

**Example**:
- "If it snows, then I will study discrete math."
- "If I study discrete math, I will get an A."
- $\therefore$ "If it snows, I will get an A."

---

### 2.4 Disjunctive Syllogism (析取三段论)

$$\begin{array}{c}
p \lor q \\
\neg p \\
\hline
\therefore q
\end{array}$$

- 对应的永真式: $((p \lor q) \land \neg p) \to q$

**Example**:
- "I will study discrete math or I will study English literature."
- "I will not study discrete math."
- $\therefore$ "I will study English literature."

---

### 2.5 Addition (附加律)

$$\begin{array}{c}
p \\
\hline
\therefore p \lor q
\end{array}$$

- 对应的永真式: $p \to (p \lor q)$

**Example**:
- "I will study discrete math."
- $\therefore$ "I will study discrete math or I will visit Las Vegas."

---

### 2.6 Simplification (化简律)

$$\begin{array}{c}
p \land q \\
\hline
\therefore p
\end{array}$$

- 对应的永真式: $(p \land q) \to p$

**Example**:
- "I will study discrete math and English literature."
- $\therefore$ "I will study discrete math."

---

### 2.7 Conjunction (合取律)

$$\begin{array}{c}
p \\
q \\
\hline
\therefore p \land q
\end{array}$$

- 对应的永真式: $(p \land q) \to (p \land q)$ (即 $p \to (q \to (p \land q))$)

**Example**:
- "I will study discrete math."
- "I will study English literature."
- $\therefore$ "I will study discrete math and I will study English literature."

---

### 2.8 Resolution (消解律)

$$\begin{array}{c}
p \lor q \\
\neg p \lor r \\
\hline
\therefore q \lor r
\end{array}$$

- 对应的永真式: $((p \lor q) \land (\neg p \lor r)) \to (q \lor r)$
- 在 AI 和 Prolog 中有重要应用。

**Example**:
- $p$: "I will study discrete math."
- $q$: "I will study databases."
- $r$: "I will study English literature."
- "I will not study discrete math or I will study English literature." ($\neg p \lor r$)
- "I will study discrete math or I will study databases." ($p \lor q$)
- $\therefore$ "I will study databases or I will study English literature." ($q \lor r$)

## 3. Building Valid Arguments (构建有效论证)

**步骤**:
1. 将自然语言命题翻译为命题逻辑符号。
2. 列出所有前提。
3. 逐行应用推理规则，直到得到结论。

### Example: Swimsuit Problem

**Premises**:
- "It is not sunny this afternoon and it is colder than yesterday."
- "We will go swimming only if it is sunny."
- "If we do not go swimming, then we will take a canoe trip."
- "If we take a canoe trip, then we will be home by sunset."

**Conclusion**: "We will be home by sunset."

**Solution**:
1. 定义命题变量:
   - $p$: "It is sunny this afternoon."
   - $q$: "It is colder than yesterday."
   - $r$: "We will go swimming."
   - $s$: "We will take a canoe trip."
   - $t$: "We will be home by sunset."

2. 翻译前提:
   - $\neg p \land q$
   - $r \to p$ (注意: "$p$ only if $q$" $\equiv$ $p \to q$, 所以 "go swimming only if sunny" = $r \to p$)
   - $\neg r \to s$
   - $s \to t$

3. 构建有效论证:

| 步骤 | 命题 | 推理规则 |
|:---:|:----|:--------:|
| 1 | $\neg p \land q$ | Premise |
| 2 | $\neg p$ | Simplification from (1) |
| 3 | $r \to p$ | Premise |
| 4 | $\neg r$ | Modus Tollens from (2)(3) |
| 5 | $\neg r \to s$ | Premise |
| 6 | $s$ | Modus Ponens from (4)(5) |
| 7 | $s \to t$ | Premise |
| 8 | $t$ | Modus Ponens from (6)(7) |

$\therefore$ "We will be home by sunset."

## 4. Rules of Inference for Quantified Statements

### 4.1 Universal Instantiation (UI, 全称实例)

$$\begin{array}{c}
\forall x P(x) \\
\hline
\therefore P(c)
\end{array}$$

(其中 $c$ 是论域中的某个元素)

**Example**: "All dogs are cuddly." $\implies$ "Fido is cuddly."

---

### 4.2 Universal Generalization (UG, 全称引入)

$$\begin{array}{c}
P(c) \text{ for an arbitrary } c \\
\hline
\therefore \forall x P(x)
\end{array}$$

(其中 $c$ 是论域中的任意元素，在数学证明中经常隐式使用)

---

### 4.3 Existential Instantiation (EI, 存在实例)

$$\begin{array}{c}
\exists x P(x) \\
\hline
\therefore P(c) \text{ for some element } c
\end{array}$$

(将存在性陈述变为某个具体元素的性质，$c$ 为尚未使用过的符号)

**Example**: "There is someone who got an A in the course." $\implies$ "Let's call her $a$ and say that $a$ got an A."

---

### 4.4 Existential Generalization (EG, 存在引入)

$$\begin{array}{c}
P(c) \text{ for some } c \\
\hline
\therefore \exists x P(x)
\end{array}$$

**Example**: "Michelle got an A in the class." $\implies$ "Someone got an A in the class."

---

## 5. Using Rules of Inference with Quantifiers

### Example 1: Socrates 论证

**Premises**:
- $\forall x (M(x) \to L(x))$ ("Every man has two legs")
- $M(\text{John Smith})$ ("John Smith is a man")

**Conclusion**: $L(\text{John Smith})$ ("John Smith has two legs")

**Valid Argument**:
| 步骤 | 命题 | 推理规则 |
|:---:|:----|:--------:|
| 1 | $\forall x (M(x) \to L(x))$ | Premise |
| 2 | $M(\text{JS}) \to L(\text{JS})$ | UI from (1) |
| 3 | $M(\text{JS})$ | Premise |
| 4 | $L(\text{JS})$ | Modus Ponens from (2)(3) |

### Example 2: Someone passed the exam but hasn't read the book

**Premises**:
- "A student in this class has not read the book." ($\exists x (C(x) \land \neg B(x))$)
- "Everyone in this class passed the first exam." ($\forall x (C(x) \to P(x))$)

**Conclusion**: "Someone who passed the first exam has not read the book." ($\exists x (P(x) \land \neg B(x))$)

**Valid Argument**:
| 步骤 | 命题 | 推理规则 |
|:---:|:----|:--------:|
| 1 | $\exists x (C(x) \land \neg B(x))$ | Premise |
| 2 | $C(a) \land \neg B(a)$ | EI from (1) |
| 3 | $C(a)$ | Simplification from (2) |
| 4 | $\forall x (C(x) \to P(x))$ | Premise |
| 5 | $C(a) \to P(a)$ | UI from (4) |
| 6 | $P(a)$ | Modus Ponens from (3)(5) |
| 7 | $\neg B(a)$ | Simplification from (2) |
| 8 | $P(a) \land \neg B(a)$ | Conjunction from (6)(7) |
| 9 | $\exists x (P(x) \land \neg B(x))$ | EG from (8) |

---

## 6. Universal Modus Ponens (全称假言推理)

结合 Universal Instantiation 和 Modus Ponens 为一体:

$$\begin{array}{c}
\forall x (P(x) \to Q(x)) \\
P(a) \\
\hline
\therefore Q(a)
\end{array}$$

**Example** (Socrates):
- $\forall x (\text{Man}(x) \to \text{Mortal}(x))$
- $\text{Man}(\text{Socrates})$
- $\therefore \text{Mortal}(\text{Socrates})$

## Summary & Exam Tips (小结与考点提示)

| 考点 | 说明 |
|:----|:-----|
| **8 条推理规则** | Modus Ponens, Modus Tollens, Hypothetical Syllogism, Disjunctive Syllogism, Addition, Simplification, Conjunction, Resolution |
| **量词推理规则** | UI (全称实例), UG (全称引入), EI (存在实例), EG (存在引入) |
| **论证构建** | 将 premise 用符号表示，逐条应用推理规则，一步步推导出结论 |
| **全称假言推理** | $\forall x(P(x)\to Q(x))$, $P(a)$ $\implies$ $Q(a)$ — 最常用的推理模式 |

**推理规则速查表**:

| 规则 | 前提 | 结论 |
|:----|:----|:----:|
| Modus Ponens | $p$, $p\to q$ | $q$ |
| Modus Tollens | $\neg q$, $p\to q$ | $\neg p$ |
| Hypothetical Syllogism | $p\to q$, $q\to r$ | $p\to r$ |
| Disjunctive Syllogism | $p\lor q$, $\neg p$ | $q$ |
| Addition | $p$ | $p\lor q$ |
| Simplification | $p\land q$ | $p$ |
| Conjunction | $p$, $q$ | $p\land q$ |
| Resolution | $p\lor q$, $\neg p\lor r$ | $q\lor r$ |

**易错提醒**:
- Modus Tollens 是 $\neg q$ 和 $p\to q$ 推出 $\neg p$，**不是** $\neg p$ 和 $p\to q$ 推出 $\neg q$ (否定前件谬误!)
- EI 引入的 $c$ 必须是**未曾用过的新符号**
- UI 和 EG 更安全，EI 和 UG 使用时有约束条件
- "A only if B" $\equiv$ $A \to B$，在构建论证时特别注意
